About Us

Building high-grade supply, marine infrastructure, and engineered operations with a future-forward standard.

Mining Dredging Marine Construction

DTC is a mining, dredging, and construction company that supplies to both local and export markets with high regards to product grade and quality. We also focus on marine construction, reclamation, and dredging.

DTC’s blueprint is to grow its investments in the mining and infrastructure sectors across the country as well as overseas.

The company was established with the objective of acquiring, developing, and expanding mining and marine construction operations on the Asian continent to effectuate development, cultivate growth, and formulate definitive high-value returns.

DTC is recognized by international companies and clients, supporting DTC’s projects through engineering plans, technical details, training, and equipment.
